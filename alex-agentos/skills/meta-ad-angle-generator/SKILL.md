# Meta Ad Angle Generator

Creative is the targeting. Produce distinct ANGLES (different reasons to care), not variations of one ad. Always anchor to the client's real offer and the landing/WhatsApp destination.

## Inputs
- Offer + price + buying cycle (impulse vs considered).
- The strategic angle from `competitor-scan` if available (say what nobody else says).
- Brand voice; destination (landing page / WhatsApp / booking).

## Generate
1. **Angles (4-6, distinct)** — each a different emotional driver: pain/relief, desire/aspiration, fear/urgency (honest), social proof, curiosity/pattern-break, offer/deal. One line each + who it hits.
2. **Hook bank (10-15)** — the first 1s line/visual. Thumb-stoppers. Mix question, bold claim, relatable moment, visual surprise.
3. **Reel scripts (2-3)** — 15-30s, scene-by-scene: hook (0-2s) → context → payoff/offer → CTA. Filmable with a phone.
4. **Thumbnail / static concepts (3-4)** — for feed; text-safe, one message.
5. **Primary text (3-5)** — written out; first line = second hook; end = one CTA.
6. **Headlines (5-8)** — short, offer- or outcome-led.
7. **Testing matrix** — angle × format × offer; what varies, kill/scale rule (e.g., kill below X CTR or above Y CPL after Z spend).
8. **Retargeting ideas** — for engaged/site-visitors/lead-not-booked: objection-handlers, proof, urgency, offer bump.

## Output
Deliver the 8 sections above, ready to hand to production (`image-video-prompt-engineer` for generated assets) and to the ad account.

## Never
- Produce 6 versions of the same angle.
- Write hooks that don't stop the thumb.
- Detach the creative from the offer and the destination page.
