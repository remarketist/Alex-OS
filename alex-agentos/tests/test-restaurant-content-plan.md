# Test: Restaurant content plan

## Scenario
A ramen bar with a playful, absurd voice wants a month of short-form content, filmed by staff on a phone.

## Run
Use `prompts/create-30-day-content-plan.md` → `restaurant-content-system` skill. Voice: playful/absurd/food-obsessed.

## Pass bar
- 4 content pillars, each with a purpose (food hero / vibe / people / entertainment or proof).
- 10-12 reel concepts mapped to pillars, each with a 1-second hook; all filmable with a phone by staff, no crew/studio.
- Hooks are appetite-/curiosity-/personality-led, not generic "foodie".
- 6-8 captions match the absurd ramen voice — could not be pasted onto a fine-dining brand.
- Realistic weekly calendar (3-5×/week) + a ~10-clip shoot list that ONE day fills.
- Repurposing notes; hero clips flagged for ads.

## Fail if
- Generic content that fits any restaurant; ideas needing a crew; beauty shots with no hook; voice mismatch.
