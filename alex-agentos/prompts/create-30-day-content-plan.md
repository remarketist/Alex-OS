# Prompt: Create a 30-day content plan

---
Create a 30-day short-form content plan for <CLIENT NAME>. Use `social-content-agent` (for restaurants/bars/venues use the `restaurant-content-system` skill). Read `brand-voice.md` and `content-examples.md` first.

Deliver: content strategy + the platform behavior we're exploiting, 4 content pillars, 10-12 reel concepts mapped to pillars (each with a 1-second hook and why it stops the scroll — all filmable with a phone), a hook bank (12-15), 6-8 captions in the exact brand voice, a realistic weekly calendar (which pillar posts which day), a shoot list of ~10 clips that ONE shoot day can capture to fill the plan, and repurposing notes. Flag hero clips to send to `meta-ad-angle-generator`.
